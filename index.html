Simple app to check your Mac Battery health.

healthi shows a small icon in your topbar with the current battery health of your mac, which is the percentage of your battery capacity compared to the original one.

  • 90% - 100%: Battery is good
  • 80% - 90%: Battery is ok but it should be replaced soon
  • 0% - 80%: Battery needs to be replaced

Download

There are two options to install this app:

  1. Without Homebrew:

    Check the realeases page for binaries.

  2. With Homebrew:

    If you have Homebrew with the Cask extension, it's as simple as running:

    brew cask install healthi
